Why Us?

Connect, Create, Cuisine

An enthusiastic and passionate cook from the moment she put a pan on the stove, Beverley Sutherland-Smith's hobby of cooking and love of food quickly turned into a business. Opened in 1967, the Beverly-Sutherland Smith Cooking School is one of Australia's longest established cooking schools. With over 40 years of experience in food consultancy and menu planning, Beverly has published over 27 cookbooks, written for various mass-distributed publications, and is also the recipent of numerous commendations such as the Australian Golden Book award.
